COST PLAN:

For foundation workers, skilled structure workers will work 19 days. They will be four in number, with an annual salary of 650.
Mason workers will be required to work for 40 days for skilled masons and 50 days for helpers. Their starting salaries
will be 650 and 450 pesos, respectively. Four skilled masons and four assistants will be present. Painters will be paid ₱500 for
21 days of work. There will be three of them. Three plumbers will work for 20 days and be paid ₱600. 1 foreman will work for
60 days and be paid ₱800. One project manager will work for 60 days and be paid ₱510.
